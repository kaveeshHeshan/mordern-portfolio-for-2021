    Cost Effective and time saving Effeicient House construction web application - 4th year Research Project

    This reaserch is mainly foucused on how to reduce the unncesary costs as well as the time and money watage. In this research carpenter and mason is the component handled by me. Nowadays people are having so many difficulties to find professional and responsible carpenter and a mason to build their dream house. As well as by finding these service providers (Carpenter and mason), most of the people will have to spend more money and time for this even though they have a tight schedule for their other activities.
